# Discovery — DoneFast Landing Page

Date: 2026-04-25

## 1. The Product
DoneFast is a productized AI implementation retainer. The buyer is a company owner who knows they should be using AI in their business but doesn't know *what* to implement, *how* to implement it, or *who* to trust. They've been quoted $5,000+ by agencies for a single n8n automation, and feel like everyone in the AI-implementation space is overcharging, overpromising, and underdelivering. After signing up: they hand DoneFast a problem (or a vague "we waste time on X"), DoneFast handles the entire AI implementation — workflows, agents, internal tools, integrations — for a flat $1,250/month with a 75%-back-after-first-week guarantee.

## 2. The Buyer

**Specifics:**
- Companies that have been operating for **5+ years** (proven, profitable, not pre-revenue)
- **Non-technical founders / no technical team in-house**
- Industry: **traditional businesses** — HVAC, plumbing, brick-and-mortar service businesses, AND non-tech online businesses
- Explicitly NOT: tech companies, software-native businesses, marketing agencies, course sellers, e-commerce brands (these are NOT the ICP, despite what current copy implies)

**Situation when they look:**
- They've heard "you need AI in your business" everywhere — newsletters, X, podcasts, conferences
- They know they're losing time to manual work but don't know what to automate first
- They've gotten one or two agency quotes — $5k–$15k for what feels like a small build
- They suspect they're being overcharged but have no benchmark

**Triggered search by:**
- A quote that felt insulting
- A competitor visibly shipping AI-driven outcomes
- Time pressure: an ops bottleneck they personally are stuck doing

## 3. Conversion Goal
- **Primary CTA:** Book a 15-min call (Cal.com)
- Secondary CTA: none — page funnels to one action
- No self-serve checkout for now
- Commitment: ~15 min of buyer's time, no money upfront

## 4. Traffic & Awareness
- **Sources (current):** Cold outbound + Facebook ads
- **Dominant awareness level:** Problem-aware → "I know I need AI in my business, I don't know how"
- **Implications:** Page must do problem-validation early ("you know you need this"), then collapse the buyer's confusion about *what* to do, then offer DoneFast as the de-risked path. Heavy education NOT needed (buyer knows the problem). Heavy *trust-building* IS needed (buyer's been burned/quoted by agencies).

## 5. Alternatives

**Direct competitors:**
- AI implementation agencies (charging $5k+ per build)
- n8n / Zapier-focused automation agencies
- "Zapier Experts" / "Make Partners"

**Adjacent solutions:**
- Hiring a freelancer (Upwork, Toptal)
- Hiring an in-house automation engineer
- Buying a horizontal AI tool (Zapier itself, Make.com, etc.) and DIY-ing it
- Sending a team member to a course / "AI workshop"

**Status quo:**
- Doing it manually
- Telling themselves "we'll figure it out next quarter"
- Bookmarking AI tools they never implement

**Most-chosen alternative (best guess, to validate in research):** Zapier-direction (DIY) for those who try, agencies for those who give up DIYing. Most realistic competitor for the page is "the agency that quoted you $7,500."

## 6. Existing Assets Received
- None. No client list, no sales call notes, no testimonials, no objection log.
- The current website copy exists but is being deprecated (do NOT mine `copy.md` — outdated and being replaced).
- Research must come from forums, reviews, and competitor mining.

## 7. Pricing & Offer
- **Price:** $1,250 / month, flat (UPDATED from $750)
- One active build at a time, unlimited queue
- 3-5 day average delivery per build
- Pause anytime, cancel anytime
- Buyer owns all code/workflows from day one
- **Guarantee:** 75% refund if they don't like it after the first week
- Senior engineer review on every build

## 8. Voice & Constraints
- Direct, anti-agency, slightly snarky, blue-collar engineering tone
- Not corporate, not "transform your business"
- Banned words: synergy, unlock, transform, leverage, empower, supercharge, revolutionize
- Approved tone reference: current hero copy ("Stop hiring agencies. Start shipping automations.") — keep this energy

## 9. Founder Story (for use as proof / origin section)
"I started DoneFast because I'm an AI nerd. I've been going down this rabbit hole for years. Now the world's catching up — every business owner knows they need AI in their business — but they haven't spent the last few years living inside it like I have."

This is the hook for trust: not "we're an agency that pivoted to AI," but "I've been doing this since before it was a trend."
